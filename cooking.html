I learn most of the recipes from my mom, mainly Cantonese cuisine. These recipes are quick, easy and delicious. After a long day from work, it is important to have a good meal for the family.

Steamed beef with preserved pickles

Ingredients:

1 pack - Preserved pickles (mix with 3 TSP of peanut oil)
1/2 pound - beef (rib eye, sliced)

Beef Marinade:

1 TSP - sugar
1 1/2 TBSP - soy sauce
1 TSP - corn starch
Peanut oil - splash

Instructions:

1. Marinade the beef for 15 min
2. Mix beef and preserved pickles
3. Steam for 10 minutes on high heat

Borsch Soup

Ingredients:

2 - tomatoes (diced)
2 - Yukon gold potatoes
1 - yellow onion (large)
3 - carrot (small)
1/4 pound - beef shank (牛𦟌)
1/2 - cabbage
1 can (8oz) - tomato paste
8 quarts - water

Instructions:

1. Boil water in a large pot on high heat
2. Put all ingredients, turn the heat to medium-high, cook for 1 ½ hours
3. Put salt to taste

Steamed Cod Fish with Black Beans （豆豉蒸银雪鱼） Ingredients: ½ pound of cod fish 1 TBSP – black beans 1 TSP – sugar 1 TSP – peanut oil 1 TSP – chicken bullion 1 TSP – salt 3 sprigs - green onions 2 TBSP - Steamed fish soy sauce Instructions: 1. Soak black beans in water for 15 mins. Then drain it. 2. Ground black beans, mix with sugar and oil. 3. Coat fish with salt and pepper. Place fish on the plate. Put mixed black beans on top. 4. Steam fish for 15 mins on high heat. 5. Pour Steamed fish soy sauce to the plate
