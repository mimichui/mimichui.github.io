After a long day from work, it is important to have a good meal for the family. These recipes are mostly Traditional Cantonese dishes. They are quick, easy and delicious.

Steamed beef with preserved pickles

Ingredients:

1 pack - Preserved pickles (mix with 3 TSP of peanut oil)
1/2 pound - beef (rib eye, sliced)

Beef Marinade:

1 TSP - sugar
1 1/2 TBSP - soy sauce
1 TSP - corn starch
Peanut oil - splash

Instructions:

1. Marinade the beef for 15 min
2. Mix beef and preserved pickles
3. Steam for 10 minutes on high heat

Borsch Soup

Ingredients:

2 - tomatoes (diced)
2 - Yukon gold potatoes
1 - yellow onion (large)
3 - carrot (small)
1/4 pound - beef shank
1/2 - cabbage
1 can (8oz) - tomato paste
8 quarts - water

Instructions:

1. Boil water in a large pot on high heat
2. Put all ingredients, turn the heat to medium-high, cook for 1 1/2 hours
3. Put salt to taste
