My Hometown

My Name is Mimi. I was born in Hong Kong. I am able to speak fluent English and Chinese. After graduating from college, I started to think there should be a place where I could explore and find excitement in my life. Then I found BOSTON. A city almost 8,000 miles away from my hometown.

My Life in Boston

When I first came to Boston, I found this city full of energy. People were jogging and cycling along Charles River. Young talented musicians were playing instruments in Harvard Square. I immediately fell in love with it. I started by taking an internship at a company in Cambridge. Then the internship turned into a permanent job.

My Hobbies

I love hiking. It's always a challenge to hike up those 4,000-foot mountains in New Hampshire. I feel accomplished when I stand at the summit and look down. Photography is one of my hobbies as well. I can show people how I see this world. Guess where I took this picture.

My Family

Now, I settle down in Greater Boston with my family. I love dogs, but my husband likes cats. We are still deciding what type of pet we'll get (or may be, it will never happen). We'll see!
